About This Podcast

Join our host and design expert as they explore the fundamentals of bio-inspired technology through an engaging conversation. This podcast covers:

  • What biomimicry is and how it guides innovation
  • Core principles of bio-inspired design (circular systems, self-healing materials)
  • How to apply biomimicry in technology development
  • Real-world examples and applications
  • Best practices for sustainable bio-tech solutions

Perfect for learning on the go! Listen while commuting, exercising, or whenever you prefer audio learning.
