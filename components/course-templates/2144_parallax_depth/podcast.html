About This Podcast

Join our host and design expert as they explore the fundamentals of parallax design through an engaging conversation. This podcast covers:

  • What parallax scrolling is and how it creates depth
  • Core principles of parallax design (layered movement, depth perception)
  • How to implement parallax effects in web design
  • Performance optimization techniques for smooth scrolling
  • Best practices for creating immersive parallax experiences

Perfect for learning on the go! Listen while commuting, exercising, or whenever you prefer audio learning.
